The Artist

Painting worlds

I am Shambhavi Kulkarni — a painter drawn to the quiet intensity of colour and form. My work explores the boundary between the seen and the felt, crafting visual spaces that invite you to linger and discover.

Rooted in classical technique yet unafraid of abstraction, each painting is a journey — from first sketch to final glazing — a meditation on light, emotion, and the stories our eyes tell.

My Approach

The Process

01

Conception

Every work begins in silence — in a walk, a dream, a conversation half-remembered. I sketch compulsively, filling journals with gestures and colour notes until the image clarifies itself.

02

Construction

Layer by layer the painting builds. Underpainting establishes the tonal architecture. Then colour arrives in glazes — translucent, luminous — each pass revealing what the last concealed.

03

Completion

The final stage is as much about restraint as addition. Knowing when a painting is finished — trusting the silence — is the hardest and most important part of the practice.
